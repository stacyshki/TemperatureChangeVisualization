Data Description

The United Nations' FAOSTAT Temperature Change dataset provides country-level records of surface temperature change across the globe, updated annually. Covering the years from 1961 to 2023, this dataset includes monthly, seasonal, and annual temperature anomalies—how much warmer or cooler it was compared to a historical average between 1951 and 1980.

The data originates from the publicly available GISTEMP dataset, maintained by NASA’s Goddard Institute for Space Studies (NASA-GISS). It provides standardized information for countries and regions, following international definitions for land area and environmental statistics frameworks.

The visualizations on this site use this dataset to explore how temperatures have changed over time and across different parts of the world. From line graphs to spirals and interactive maps, these charts aim to help people everywhere better understand the scope of global warming—and how the planet’s climate has evolved over recent decades.
